Sunday, September 28, 2008

Autumn Aloha! Lua`u Fundraiser

Experience the Aloha of a Polynesian Paradise! Break out the Aloha Shirts and muumuus to join us for an afternoon of family fun and entertainment! There'll be Hawaiian-themed games, raffles, and prizes for kids and adults, and mouth-watering foods of Hawai`i and Samoa served buffet style. Members of Hui Lehua will whisk you away to a Pacific paradise as they present a Polynesian program featuring old and new songs and dances from Hawai`i and Tahiti, joined by friends and `ohana.
Portion of proceeds to benefit the Bobby Benson Center in Kahuku, Hawai`i.

Time: 1:00 to 4 p.m.
Place: American Legion Club, Post #34 / 176 Main St / Plaistow, NH 03865
Tickets: Keiki (Children) under age 6, Free
$10, Keiki ages 6 ~12
$20, 12 years to Adult
$15, Kupuna (Seniors) over 65

For more information, to purchase tickets, or make group reservations, contact: Nancy Griffin, at hyetti@juno.com or call her at 603-642-8509.

Hui Lehua 'O Kamawila is a collaborative hula group dedicated to preserving and sharing Hawai`ian dance traditions in New England.

The dancers take their name from the lehua flower which blooms in adverse conditions, including cooling lava flows.

 

To contact Hui Lehua for a performance or lua`u---or for more information about the group---email huilehua AT gmail DOT com.
